📊 Integrated Sales & Production Intelligence Dashboard
A comprehensive Power BI business intelligence solution designed to bridge the gap between commercial performance and operational efficiency. This project features two primary reporting layers: Sales Analytics and Production Monitoring.

🚀 Key Modules
1. Sales Performance Dashboard
Focuses on revenue growth, market penetration, and customer behavior.

Core KPIs: Total Sales ($277.70bn), Profit Margin (9.58%), and Total Orders (3.89M).

Trend Tracking: Monthly and Quarterly sales volume analysis to identify seasonal patterns.

Segmentation: Breakdown of revenue by Sales Channel (Online vs. Retail), Payment Method, and Product Category (Electronics, Furniture, Food, etc.).

Geographic Insights: Regional performance distribution across East, South, North, and West.

2. Production & Operations Dashboard
Focuses on manufacturing efficiency, resource allocation, and quality control.

Operational KPIs: Monitoring Shift Hours, Production Volume, and Workforce Size.

Efficiency Metrics: Machine efficiency analysis categorized by energy efficiency levels (High, Medium, Low).

Quality Control: Analysis of "Positive" vs. "Negative" production by line and Quality Risk Level tracking.

Cost Analysis: Raw material cost distribution by department and production flexibility volume.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Tool: Power BI Desktop

Data Modeling: Star Schema / DAX (Calculated measures for Profit Margins and Production Efficiency)

Visuals: KPI Cards, Donut Charts, Area Charts, Treemaps, and Ribbon Charts.

💡 Key Business Insights
Sales Mix: Electronics dominate the product category by total sales value.

Payment Trends: Credit Card and Bank Transfer are the preferred payment methods, indicating a highly digitized customer base.

Operational Risk: The "Production" module identifies specific lines where negative production correlates with high quality-risk levels, allowing for targeted maintenance.

Profitability: Despite high sales, the 9.58% profit margin suggests a need for cost optimization in specific underperforming regions.

Developed as a Data Analytics portfolio project to demonstrate proficiency in Power BI and end-to-end business reporting.
